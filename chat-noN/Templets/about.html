About Us

Welcome to our University Students Chatbot project. This chatbot is designed to assist university students with their queries, provide emotional support, and guide them with academic-related help. Our goal is to make student life easier and more connected through AI-powered conversation tools.

This chatbot can analyze student emotions based on text inputs and provide tailored responses to improve engagement. By focusing on emotional intelligence, we aim to create a supportive and accessible system for students.

The technology stack includes Natural Language Processing (NLP) and deep learning to understand user inputs. We have utilized Python for the backend and a dynamic, user-friendly interface for seamless interaction with students.

This is our **About Page**! Our project is centered around providing a virtual assistant that empowers students by offering insights and solving problems in real time. Whether it's scheduling, academic support, or mental health support, the chatbot is versatile and adaptable. Join us in creating a smarter, more connected university experience!
